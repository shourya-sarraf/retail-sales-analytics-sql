# Metric Definitions

## Gross Sales
Gross Sales represent the total revenue generated before applying any discounts.

Formula:
Gross Price × Sold Quantity

---

## Pre-Invoice Discount
A discount applied before the invoice is generated.
This is usually negotiated at the customer or contract level.

---

## Post-Invoice Discount
Discounts and deductions applied after invoicing.
Includes promotional discounts and other deductions.

---

## Net Sales
Net Sales represent the actual revenue earned after applying all discounts.

Formula:
Gross Sales × (1 − Pre-Invoice Discount) × (1 − Post-Invoice Discount)

---

## Market Share
Market Share represents the contribution of a customer or market to total net sales.

Formula:
(Customer Net Sales ÷ Total Net Sales) × 100
